TGS Native Pollinator Seed Packets 2023

Thanks for picking up a native pollinator seed packet.

First, thanks to everyone who give me permission to harvest seeds from their garden, or donated seeds to the cause!

Seeds in these packets

In no particular order:

  • Star tickseed
  • Lanceleaf Coreopsis
  • Columbine
  • Late purple aster
  • Prairie blazingstar
  • Cliff goldenrod
  • Hairy mountain mint
  • Bee balm (various)
  • Coneflower (various)

How to plant

  • Generally, these plants are going to do better in medium to full light.
  • Generally, these plants benefit greatly from cold moist stratification. Meaning get them on/in the ground in Dec/Jan if you want them to come up this spring.
  • Generally, these plants need direct soil contact, e.g. plat them on/in bare dirt, not sprinkled over mulch.
