TGS Native Pollinator Seed Packets 2023

Thanks to everyone who give me permission to harvest seeds from their garden, or donated seeds! All the seed in these packets was collected in TGS, are all from plants native to Missouri, and are all pollinator-friendly.

Seeds in these packets

In no particular order:

  • Star tickseed
  • Lanceleaf Coreopsis
  • Columbine
  • Late purple aster
  • New england aster
  • Prairie blazingstar
  • Cliff goldenrod
  • Hairy mountain mint
  • Bee balm (various)
  • Coneflower (various)

...plus some others in very small quantities

How to plant

Generally:

  • these plants are going to do better in spots with medium to full sun. I would not plant them somewhere with low to no sun.
  • thse seeds will need cold moist stratification. Meaning get them on/in the ground in Dec/Jan if you want them to come up this spring.
  • these seeds will need direct soil contact to seed successfully, e.g. plant them on/in bare dirt, not sprinkled over mulch.
