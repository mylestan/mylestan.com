TGS Native Pollinator Seed Packets 2023

Thanks for picking up a native pollinator seed packet.

First, thanks to everyone who give me permission to harvest seeds from their garden, or donated seeds to the cause! All the seeds in these packets was collected in TGS, are all plants native to Missouri, and are all pollinator-friendly.

Seeds in these packets

In no particular order (not all packets contain all these):

  • Star tickseed
  • Lanceleaf Coreopsis
  • Columbine
  • Late purple aster
  • New england aster
  • Prairie blazingstar
  • Cliff goldenrod
  • Hairy mountain mint
  • Bee balm (various)
  • Coneflower (various)

How to plant

Generally, for these plants:

  • They're going to do better in spots with medium to full sun. I would not plant them somewhere with low to no sun.
  • They'll need cold moist stratification. Meaning get them on/in the ground in Dec/Jan if you want them to come up this spring.
  • Generally, these plants need direct soil contact, e.g. plat them on/in bare dirt, not sprinkled over mulch.
